## Core Framework: Promotion Gap Analysis
**Source:** PM career frameworks
**When to use:** Planning promotions in corporate PM roles

**Typical PM Levels:**
```
Associate PM → PM → Senior PM → Staff/Principal → Director
```

**Gap Analysis:**
- Scope (size of impact)
- Complexity (difficulty of problems)
- Independence (guidance needed)
- Leadership (influence on others)
