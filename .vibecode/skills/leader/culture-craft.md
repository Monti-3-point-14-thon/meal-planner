**One-line description:** Design team culture and excellence standards (primarily relevant when building teams).

## Core Framework: Culture as Competitive Advantage
**Source:** Stripe, HubSpot
**When to use:** Building or leading teams

**Key Principles:**
1. Define what excellence means
2. Hire for culture add (not fit)
3. Create reinforcing rituals
4. Make values visible

## Solo Founder Context

**Note:** NOT relevant for solo founders without teams.

**More relevant when:**

- Building team culture
- Scaling organization
