## Core Framework: 7 Sources of Power
**Source:** Jeffrey Pfeffer
**When to use:** Building influence without formal authority

**The 7 Sources:**
1. **Expertise** - Being the expert
2. **Resources** - Controlling budget/headcount
3. **Relationships** - Network and connections
4. **Information** - Knowing what others don't
5. **Position** - Formal authority
6. **Charisma** - Personal magnetism
7. **Will** - Persistence and determination

---

## Solo Founder Context

**Note:** This skill is less relevant for solo founders without teams.

**More relevant when:**
- Hiring first employees/contractors
- Working with advisors/investors
- Partnering with other founders
- Growing into team leadership

**For solo work:** Focus on **Expertise**, **Information**, and **Will** as your primary sources of power with users and partners.
