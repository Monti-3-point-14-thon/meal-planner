## Core Framework: Radical Candor
**Source:** Kim Scott
**When to use:** Giving or receiving feedback

**The Formula:**
```
Care Personally + Challenge Directly = Radical Candor
```

**SBI Feedback Structure:**
- **Situation:** When/where it happened
- **Behavior:** What they did (observable)
- **Impact:** How it affected you/team

**Example:**
```
"In yesterday's meeting (Situation),
when you interrupted me twice (Behavior),
it made me lose my train of thought (Impact)."
```
